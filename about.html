Make keeping up a habit.

Stop waiting for chance encounters and status updates to remind you who matters. Be proactive with Socialfly reminders, and we'll email you or notify you on Facebook when it's time to talk. It's that easy.

Take note, quickly.

Remember important details and plan your next steps, without switching between different apps and menus.

Remember each relationship.

Get up to date quickly with a private feed of important details about each relationship, and watch your relationships evolve as you identify and follow through with social opportunities. Additional details are loaded from Facebook to start you off with the basics, and we'll soon let you import data from your address book and other sources.

Private and secure.

Whereas most web services are about sharing information between people, Socialfly is completely private, and cannot be seen by anybody but you. If you want to write why you don't like somebody, that's fine - they'll never know.

Where your friends live.

Traveling somewhere? Use Socialfly to remember where your friends live, so you can quickly figure out who to visit.

Get the big picture.

Between casual acquaintances, professional contacts, and all your other friends, you probably want to remember many things about many people. Looking this up would normally be time consuming, but with Socialfly you can get perspective on all your relationships - quickly. Because Socialfly's notes organize themselves, you can view every activity plan, favor, interest, and more across each relationship.

Relationships are fluid - a new acquaintance could turn into an important business contact, a good friend, or both. Using tags, you can organize your relationships across many different categories, whether you're job seeking, dating, keeping up with friends from different social groups, keeping track of skillsets, or just staying on top of your extended family.
